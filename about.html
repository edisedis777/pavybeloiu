About Me

Pavy Beloiu is from Câmpulung Muscel and descendant of 6 generations of Moldovan priests (his father, Mihai Beloiu, was the last). Passionate about art and history, he sought to bring to light events and stories lost and unknown to today's readers. He combined engineering with painting and, later, with writing and mathematics. He left for America in 2001 and misses his birthplace, but also his friends in the country.
